Integrating behavioral models, electrophysiology, and neuroimaging to bridge the gap between basic biological mechanisms and clinical psychiatric presentation.

Research Pillars

Program I

Reverse Translation

From Bedside to Bench: Modeling clinical findings in animals to dissect underlying circuitry and mechanisms.

Program II

Forward Translation

From Bench to Bedside: Translating basic research findings into clinical correlates using multimodal imaging (PET, Neuromelanin-Sensitive MRI, fMRI).

Program III

Pharmacological Mechanisms

Mechanism of Action: Elucidating the neural targets of psychoactive compounds, therapeutic agents, and non-therapeutic substances on monoaminergic systems.
